## IMMEDIATE EMERGENCY FUNDING (1-30 DAYS)

### 1. GitHub Sponsors (FASTEST)
✅ Setup time: 10 minutes
✅ No deadlines, no proposals
✅ 100% open source projects qualify
✅ Github doesn't take a cut, you get 100%
✅ Can have monthly tiers + one-time donations

**PRO TIP:** Enable this NOW. Even if you get zero dollars for 6 months, it takes 10 minutes and when someone wants to send you money it will be there.

### 2. NLNet Open Technology Fund
✅ €1,000 - €50,000 grants
✅ Deadline: Rolling (apply ANY time)
✅ Response time: 2-6 weeks
✅ No equity, no strings attached
✅ Funds anything open source (privacy, security, infrastructure, tools)
✅ **You already have a draft written for this**

### 3. Mozilla Technology Fund
✅ $5,000 - $50,000 grants
✅ Rolling applications
✅ Perfect for privacy tools, open source infrastructure, developer tools

## WHAT THEY ACTUALLY CARE ABOUT

Grant reviewers don't care about your:
- 100 page business plan
- Pitch deck
- 5 year projections

They DO care about:
✅ You are actually building this thing already
✅ It solves a real problem
✅ You won't waste their money
✅ You are not going to disappear

## QUICK PROPOSAL TEMPLATE THAT WORKS

```
Hi [Grant Team],

I'm [Your Name], creator of [Project]. [Link to github].

What it does: [One sentence].
Why it matters: [One sentence].
How much I need: $X.
What I will use it for: [3 bullet points].

That's it. No more.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

90% of grant proposals are 10 pages of garbage. Yours will be half a page and you will get funded.
